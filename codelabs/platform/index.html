1. To build your own bot, please sign up on https://hackathon.yellowmessenger.com

  1. Alternatively, you can also opt for SSO using any other account of your choice. Yellow Messenger currently supports,
  • Facebook
  • Google
  • Outlook
  • GitHub

Once Signed up, verify your account by checking your inbox for an email (check the spam folder).

  1. In the verification email, check for the bot token
  2. Copy the Token
  3. Click on ‘Get Started'

Make you've copied the bot token.

  1. On http://hackathon.yellowmessenger.com go to SandBox and click ‘New Bot'.

  1. Give a Name to your Bot, Copy past the Bot Token from the email.

And yes, your bot is ready!
